Our Story

MoolaVest is an authorized financial services firm founded in 2022 by Thakeer Moola, our youthful, lively, and enterprising CEO. He is a specialist in trading the financial markets on a regular basis. In 2018, our CEO began his forex and stock market trading career while still in high school. He traded the daily markets on a modest scale with local brokers.

Our CEO recognized that trading the financial markets is extremely difficult for individuals considering doing so for the first time and that it requires a significant amount of time as well as a psychological mindset, so he made it simple and easy for anyone interested in saving for the future.

Then our CEO, Thakeer Moola started MoolaVest, a large-cap investment business that trades on the New York Stock Exchange as well as the foreign markets which is largely foucused on islamic investments strictly, following all the guide lines of the shar'iah Law.

The goal and emphasis of MoolaVest are centered on tax efficiency and both short- and long-term investment growth.

MoolaVest is an independent and registered Brokerage in South Africa which is in line with FICA, Liscence number 56741.
